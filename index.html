Publishing Service

Turn Your Ideas Into Real Books

Have a story idea? We help children and families transform their creations into professionally formatted and globally available books. We make the process simple, fun, and empowering.

01
Get Started

Sign up and choose your publishing package. We'll guide you through everything you need to prepare.

02
Consultation

Meet with our team for a 45-minute session to structure your book and prepare your files.

03
Submit Files

Upload your manuscript, illustrations, and cover images using our clear templates.

04
Design & Format

Our team formats your book into a professional, print-ready, publishing-standard file.

05
Review & Approve

Receive a full preview and request one round of revisions before final sign-off.

06
Publish & Go Live

Your book goes live on Amazon and global platforms for the world to discover.

07
Launch Your Book

Celebrate your achievement and receive guidance on sharing your story with the world.

You keep full ownership of your work. Tiny Star simply helps bring your story to life. We make publishing simple, guided, and stress-free — so you can focus on your story.

Our Founder

Hi, I'm Dr. Joy — Founder of Tiny Star.

I was once a literature professor simply searching for the right bilingual books for my young daughter Aanya. When I couldn't find what I was looking for, I began writing down the stories she told me and turning them into books. To our surprise, our very first book became an Amazon Bestseller in 2019. Since then, we have published a series of children's books, including 2 Amazon Bestsellers. Through this journey, I clearly saw how transforming a child's ideas into a real, published book builds deep and lasting confidence.

On Thanksgiving 2024, my 9-year-old daughter Aanya and I made a clear decision: to turn this personal success into a life mission — helping children build confidence by turning their ideas into real book series.

In January 2025, I designed Write It, Draw It, Publish It — an 8-week after-school program for children ages 6–10. As a Babson Business School graduate, I integrated Babson College's Entrepreneurial Thought & Action (ETA) methodology, embedding Entrepreneurial and Executive Skills-based learning games throughout. Within its first year, the program helped over 100 children publish their first globally distributed books across 8 school sites in 4 districts of Massachusetts.

I'm truly excited to have your child and your family as part of the Tiny Star Young Author Community. I look forward to bringing the same inspiring experience that my daughter and I shared — with your child and your family!
