Publishing Service

Turn Your Ideas Into Real Books

Have a story idea? We help children and families transform their creations into professionally formatted and globally available books. We make the process simple, fun, and empowering.

1
✅
Get Started

Sign up and choose your publishing package. We'll guide you through everything you need to prepare.

2
💬
Publishing Consultation

Meet with our team for a 45-minute session to understand the process, structure your book, and prepare your files.

3
📁
Submit Your Files

Upload your manuscript, illustrations, and cover images. We provide clear templates and instructions.

4
🎨
Design & Format

Our team professionally formats your book into a print-ready, publishing-standard file.

5
👀
Review & Approve

You receive a preview and can request one round of revisions for formatting and small corrections.

6
🌐
Publish & Go Live

We publish your book and make it available on global platforms including Amazon.

7
🎉
Launch Your Book

Receive guidance on how to share your book, celebrate your achievement, and reach your readers.

🔒
You keep full ownership of your work. Tiny Star simply helps bring your story to life. We make publishing simple, guided, and stress-free — so you can focus on your story.

About Tiny Star

This is not a one-time art experience. It's a new way of growing up.

Tiny Star grows confident creators. From learning vocabulary through storytelling, to creating a real-world book, to building a book series, to developing original IP — children grow into confident creators, step by step.

We serve children aged 6–10 in Weston, Wellesley, Wayland, and Belmont — with programs, camps, workshops, and publishing services designed to nurture the creator in every child.
